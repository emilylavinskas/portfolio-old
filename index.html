Hey, I'm Emily!

As the title says above – Hey! I'm Emily. I'm a graphic designer based out of Ottawa with specialization in editorial design, print media & UI/UX design. I also have strong skills in photography and hand drawn typography. I am a passionate animal advocate, and wear black like it's going out of style. If I was in Star Wars, and had to pick between the Rebel Alliance and the Galactic Empire, I'd probably never come up with an answer. I'm a lover of autumn, making silly faces, beer and bernese mountain dogs, not in that particular order. I also ramble a lot, and suck at writing bios.
